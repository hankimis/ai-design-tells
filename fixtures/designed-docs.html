What shipped this month.

Every change to TaskFlow, written by the person who built it.

May 28
Performance

Boards open from cache first

We render the last-known board immediately, then reconcile against the server. The median open dropped from 940 ms to 180 ms on a cold tab. How we measured it.

May 14
Security

Region pinning for every workspace

You can now pin a workspace to eu-central or ap-northeast. Data, backups and audit logs stay in the region you choose, on every plan.

May 3
Integrations

Cards follow your commits

Link a branch once; the matching card moves itself through review, merged and deployed as your CI reports back. No bot comments, no manual dragging.
